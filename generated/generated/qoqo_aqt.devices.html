qoqo_aqt.devices¶

AQT Devices

Provides the devices that are used to execute quantum programs with the AQT backend. AQT devices can be physical hardware or simulators.

SimulatorDevice

AQT quantum simulator device

NoisySimulatorDevice

AQT quantum simulator device with noise model

class qoqo_aqt.devices.NoisySimulatorDevice¶

AQT quantum simulator device with noise model

Provides endpoint that receives instructions that are simulated and returns measurement results.
